Postfix Expresion Evaluator

by Cindy La and Jonathan Price for CS 310

The following operations are supported, all of which are binary (i.e. take two operands):

  • + (addition)
  • - (subtraction)
  • * (multiplication)
  • / (division)
  • % (modulo)
  • ^ (exponentiation)
Numbers can be negative or positive, as well as integers or decimals. Scientific notation is also supported, e.g. "2.1e-3". Which is 2.1 * 10^-3 or 0.0021.
